Welcome to the SIGCHICKEN Conference 2024

The Special Interest Group on Continuous High-Capacity Key Generation (SIGCHICKEN) is pleased to invite you to our annual conference, proudly affiliated with the University of Wild Chicken (Burundi).

The SIGCHICKEN Conference is a premier event for researchers, professionals, and enthusiasts in the field of high-capacity key generation. This year's theme is "Innovations in Key Generation and Cybersecurity."
